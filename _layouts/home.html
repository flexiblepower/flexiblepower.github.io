Coordination for a Smartgrid

Electricity infrastructures today are facing major challenges. Electricity use is ever-growing. More and more electricity is generated by intermittent sources: at a large-scale by off-shore wind; at a small scale by high numbers of distributed generation. This asks for a smarter grid. The PowerMatcher is the answer.

Who we are

The PowerMatcher is a smartgrid technology of TNO. The PowerMatcher was developed in cooperation with industry and research partners.

In a nutshell

A quick overview of the technology; FAQs; Experience with Virtual Power Plants and Active Distribution Management. Your fast route to the technology.

Get technical

The PowerMatcher automatically balances demand and supply in clusters of Distributed Energy Resources. The technology is based on Multi-agent Systems technology.
